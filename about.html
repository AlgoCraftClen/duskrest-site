About

The best sleep advice is boring. That's the point.

Duskrest started with a simple observation: everything that actually improves sleep is unglamorous — a dark room, a quiet room, a cool room. No pill or gadget replaces those three, and most of what's sold as "sleep tech" ignores them.

So we build affordable tools that make dark, quiet, and cool automatic. Engineered, tested, guaranteed for 30 nights — and never marketed with medical promises we can't keep.

Engineered for deeper nights.
