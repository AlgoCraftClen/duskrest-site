Our story

We got tired of being tired.

Duskrest exists because of a specific kind of night: it's 2:47am, your brain is running tomorrow's schedule on a loop, the charger light is glowing like a lighthouse, the room is two degrees too warm, and somewhere a door just closed. You've been "going to sleep" for three hours.

We went looking for the fix and found the same answer in every piece of real sleep research: there is no gadget that makes you sleep. There are only three conditions that let sleep happen — dark, quiet, and cool — and most bedrooms fail at least two of them.

Dark

Your brain reads light — any light — as "stay alert." Total blackout means you can sleep at midnight or midday.

Quiet

Ears never switch off; they wait. Steady sound gives them nothing to wait for — anywhere you unwind.

Cool

Deep sleep needs your body temperature to drop. Breathable fabric lets it happen — bed, couch, or anywhere.

So that's the whole company: seventeen carefully chosen tools, each attacking one of the three. No pills. No "sleep tech" that needs an app and a subscription. No promises we can't keep.

Every product ships with a 30-night guarantee. If your nights aren't better, you get your money back — no forms, no fight, a human answers the email.

Engineered for deeper nights. That's not a slogan — it's the job description.
